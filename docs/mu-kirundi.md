## Ingene abantu baja hanze mu buryo butandukanye
Hariho ibihugu bimwe bimwe bidatasaba ama visas ku barundi. Uburorero ni ibihugu bibanyi nka Rwanda, Tanzanie, RDC,... Hari n'ibindi bihugu vyinshi bisaba ama visas kugira umurundi aronke uburenganzira bwo kubijamwo. Visa ni agapapuro bamadika muri passeport kaguha ubwo burenganzira bwo kuja muri gihugu kanaka.

Ama visas ari ubwoko butandukanye bivyanye n'ikiba kikujanye muri ico gihugu. Bivanye n'inzira uciyemwo n'amategeko y'igihugu ushaka kujamwo, uburyo bwo kurondera visa buratandukanye. Ushobora kuja hanze:

- Kwiga
- Gutembera
- Kuramukanya
- Kwivuza
- Mu nama
- Gukora
- Gusangayo umuryango wawe
- Guhunga
- ...

Hariho abantu bakora ivyo gufasha abantu kurondera ama visas. Wokwama wibuka ko ataribo batanga ama visas kuko atangwa n'ama ambassades. Naho atari bose, birashika bamwe bamwe bakabesha kugira bibe amahera. Rero bose si abo kwizera. Mu gihe uzi igifaransa canke icongereza, ufashe umwanya ukwiye wo kugira ama recherches, bikugoye ukabanza abantu babikoze bigakunda, woshobora kuvyikorera utarinze kuriha abandi bantu babigufasha.

Ikindi co kwibuka ni uko ibintu vy'ama visas bisaba amafaranga, kandi kubera warishe amahera ntibisigura ko bitegerezwa gukunda. Muri make, visa ntibayigura, barayisaba. Iyo usavye birashobora gukunda canke bikanka. Ico ukora ni ukugerageza ukwo ushobora ugatanga ibisabwa vyose kugira dossier yawe ibe aussi convaincant que possible.

## Kwiga hanze
### Visa y'ishure
Ica mbere ni ukuronka ishure. Usaba bisanzwe nk'abandi banyeshure, ishure usavye riraba ko ryokugomwa rikaguha ikibanza mu gihe ukwije ibisabwa nk'umunyeshure. Uronse ikibanza niho utangura ivy yo gusaba visa y'abanyeshure muri buserukizi bw'ico gihugu.

Amashure yo hanze kenshi usanga azimvye. Mu gihe wewe canke umuryango wawe udafise amahera yo kukurihira, birakenewe ko urondera bourse. Amakuru ajanye n'ama bourses uyasanga ku ma sites web y'ayo mashure. Urashobora kuronka ikibanza ariko nturonke bourse. Mu gihe uterekanye neza iyo amahera y'ishure no kwibeshaho azova (kwirihira canke bourse), ambassade y'igihugu ugiyemwo ntiguha visa.

Bivanye n'ururimi ishure usavye rikoresha, kenshi barasaba ko ubanza gukora ikibazo c'igifaransa canke icongereza, ariko si igihe cose. Amashure meshi yemera imwe muri aya tests:

- Icongereza: Duolingo, TOEFL canke IELTS
- Igifaransa: TCF, TEF, DELF, DALF

### Ingene ushobora kurondera bourse
Bourses ziri kwinshi. Urashobora kuronka bourse ikurihira ibintu bikeyi canke iyikuruhira vyose (mineraval, ration, logement, billet d'avion, ...). Bumwe mu bwoko bw'ama bourses:

- Bourses zitangwa n'amashure ubwayo
- Hariho amashirahamwe ashobora kurihira abanyeshure
- Bourses ziciye muri Leta
- ...
