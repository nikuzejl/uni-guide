### U.S. Diversity Visa (Green Card Lottery)

Annual lottery that offers a limited number of U.S. permanent resident visas to applicants from eligible countries.

- [U.S. Diversity Visa program](https://dvprogram.state.gov/)

### Canada Federal Skilled Worker Program

Federal immigration pathway for skilled workers with qualifying work experience, language skills, and education.

- [Federal Skilled Worker Program](https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/express-entry)

### Quebec Regular Skilled Worker Program (PRTQ)

Quebec's immigration program for skilled workers who want to settle permanently in the province.

- [Quebec Regular Skilled Worker Program](https://www.quebec.ca/immigration/permanente/travailleurs-qualifies/programme-regulier-travailleurs-qualifies/examen)

### Express Entry

Canada's main system for skilled worker immigration. Candidates create a profile, receive a points score, and may be invited to apply for permanent residence.

- [Comprehensive Ranking System](https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/express-entry/eligibility/-criteria-comprehensive-ranking-system/grid.html) — Points grid used to rank Express Entry candidates.
- [Eligibility checker](https://onlineservices-servicesenligne.cic.gc.ca/c2c/eapp) — Online tool to see which Canadian immigration programs you may qualify for.
- [Provincial Nominee Program](https://www.canada.ca/fr/immigration-refugies-citoyennete/services/immigrer-canada/candidats-provinces.html) — Lets Canadian provinces nominate immigrants for permanent residence.
- [Language test equivalencies](https://www.canada.ca/fr/immigration-refugies-citoyennete/organisation/publications-guides/bulletins-guides-operationnels/exigences-normalisees/exigences-linguistiques/tableaux-equivalences-resultats-tests.html) — Official tables matching French and English test scores to Canadian language standards.

### Francophone Minority Communities Student Pilot

This program allows eligible French-speaking students to study in a Francophone minority community in Canada, receive settlement support during their studies, and obtain permanent residence shortly after graduation.

- [Official program page](https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/study-canada/study-permit/fmc-student-pilot.html)
